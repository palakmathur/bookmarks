Div vs Section

  • Section used for grouping thematically related content.
  • Div "has no meaning at all"
  • Is all the content related – Jeremy Kieth

Document Outlines

  • 7 tags define new outline contexts body, section, article, aside, footer, header, nav–Each of these can have their own h1 source
  • Also includes main, figure, time

Header

  • Group of introductory or navigational aids.
  • There can be more than 1 on a page
  • based on content not on position

Footer

  • represents a footer for its nearest ancestor sectioning content or sectioning root element.
  • Can be present within any of the sectioning Elements

Aside:

  • tangentially related to the content around the aside element, and which could be considered separate from that content.
  • within an article should be related to content within that element
  • Outside an aticle should be related to the whole site
